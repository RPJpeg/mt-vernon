Insurance & Payments

Mount Vernon Rehab Assoc. is dedicated to providing you with the highest quality, cost effective medical care. We accept cash, checks, MasterCard and Visa. For the most up-to-date information on our accepted private health insurance plans, please contact our office directly at (703) 664-7285.

Insurance

We accept nearly all major insurance plans including:

  • Aetna
  • CareFirst Blue Shield
  • BC Federal
  • Cigna
  • Medicare
  • Workers' Comp Accepted
  • Medicare Railroad
  • Virginia Medicaid
  • Tricare
  • United Health Care

Co-Payments

Co-payments are due at the time of service. Your insurance company requires us to collect co-payments at the time of service. Additionally, you may have coinsurance and/or deductible amounts required by your insurance carrier. Any outstanding balance on your account, after adjusting for all of your insurance's responsibilities, will be billed to you.

Additional Fees

There is an additional $20 fee for completion of forms for use outside of the patient's medical record, including:

  • Pre-Employment
  • Employment
  • Life Insurance
  • Family Medical Leave
