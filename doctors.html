Our Practice

We are a group of physicians specializing in
Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation (Physiatrists)

Weâ€™re committed to providing exceptional care to adults recovering from a wide variety of neurologic and musculoskeletal conditions.

Our doctors excel in working with patients and their families to ensure they are able to reach their goals after illness or injury. We use our expertise to devise individualized rehabilitation programs for our patients and coordinate care with therapists and other health care professionals. We also have extensive experience in evaluating patients for adaptive equipment, braces, artificial limbs, and procedures such as botulinum toxin injections for spasticity.

Our physicians are also skilled in evaluating patients with back, neck, and joint pain and in recommending therapeutic exercise programs to alleviate pain and promote improved function. We also provide superior electrodiagnostic testing to accurately diagnose muscle and nerve disorders.

Our Doctors

Roger V. Gisolfi, MD

Dr. Gisolfi is originally from New York City. After his graduation from Rutgers, he completed medical school at Georgetown, where he later served as an orthopedic surgery resident. He then served in the U.S. Navy Medical Corps, prior to completing a residency in Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation at Emory. He has served as Chairman of the Department of PM&R and the Medical Director of the rehabilitation program since 1976. He is board certified in PM&R and in Spinal Cord Injury Medicine.
